**HIVE is a social platform that works like an operating system for student life.**

We serve two user types:
- **90% Consumers**: Students who want to connect, discover events, share content, and coordinate activities

## üìê Product Architecture: The Student Autonomy Framework

### SPACES: Community Sovereignty
**Vision**: Students don't join communities, they architect them.

**Control Points**:
- Create spaces for any purpose (study groups, dorm floors, interest clubs)
- Configure space behavior (open/closed, anonymous/identified, structured/casual)
- Deploy tools that fit specific needs
- Shape culture through moderation and norms
- Evolve spaces based on member needs

**Intelligence Layer**:
- Suggests optimal meeting times based on member schedules
- Identifies engagement patterns and health metrics
- Recommends successful configurations from similar spaces
- Predicts and prevents community decay
- Facilitates cross-space learning

**Current Gap**: Students can join but not create, configure but not control, use but not innovate.

### PROFILE: Identity Sovereignty
**Vision**: Students control how they present themselves and to whom.

**Control Points**:
- Modular identity (different faces for different spaces)
- Three relationship types:
  - **Space Friends**: Automatic, contextual connections
  - **Coffee Chats**: Time-bounded trial friendships (semester-long)
  - **Direct Friends**: Intentional, permanent bonds
- Ghost Mode for complete invisibility
- Granular privacy controls per relationship type
- Data export and deletion rights

**Intelligence Layer**:
- Compatibility scores for Coffee Chat matching
- Relationship health monitoring
- Optimal introduction timing
- Social graph optimization
- Connection pattern insights

**Current Gap**: Basic profile exists but no relationship types, no intelligence, limited modularity.

### TOOLS: Solution Sovereignty
**Vision**: Students build campus utilities without code.

**The Tool Ecosystem**:

**1. Universal Foundation (6 Core Tools)**
- **Events**: Scheduling with intelligent coordination
- **Polls**: Decision making with ranked choice
- **Files**: Shared resources with version control
- **Photos**: Visual memory with auto-organization
- **Study Partners**: Academic collaboration matching
- **Anonymous**: Safe feedback and discussion

**2. Template Marketplace**
- Pre-configured tools from successful spaces
- One-click deployment with customization
- Success metrics and adoption tracking
- Creator recognition and rewards

**3. HiveLab Builder**
- Natural language ‚Üí functional tool
- "When 3+ people want food, create group order"
- "Track who owes money for shared expenses"
- "Match study partners by schedule and subject"

**Intelligence Layer**:
- Tool recommendation based on space type
- Usage pattern optimization
- Cross-space tool sharing algorithms
- Performance and adoption analytics
- Automatic tool evolution suggestions

**Current Gap**: Zero tools exist. No builder. No marketplace. Complete sovereignty blocked.

### FEED: Cultural Sovereignty
**Vision**: Students shape campus culture through intelligent aggregation.

**Control Points**:
- Algorithm transparency and customization
- Priority controls (coordination > content)
- Cross-space discovery settings
- Trend participation choices
- Cultural contribution recognition

**Intelligence Layer**:
- Coordination opportunity surfacing
- Optimal timing for maximum reach
- Trend detection and amplification
- Cultural pattern recognition
- Virality prediction

**Current Gap**: Basic feed exists but no aggregation, no intelligence, no student control.

### RITUALS: Tradition Sovereignty
**Vision**: Students create recurring rhythms that define campus culture.

**Control Points**:
- Create space-specific traditions
- Set recurrence patterns (weekly, monthly, seasonal)
- Define participation requirements
- Track tradition evolution
- Pass traditions to next generation

**Intelligence Layer**:
- Optimal scheduling for maximum participation
- Tradition health monitoring
- Cross-generational knowledge transfer
- Success pattern replication
- Cultural impact measurement

**Current Gap**: Completely unbuilt. No tradition creation or tracking.

8. **No creator recognition** - Innovation not rewarded
9. **No cultural mechanics** - Rituals don't exist
