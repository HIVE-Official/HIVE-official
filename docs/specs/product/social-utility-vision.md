# HIVE: The Social Utility Platform

## Core Vision
**"Where connections form around solving real problems together"**

HIVE isn't another social network or study app. It's where college students actually get things done - together.

## What is Social Utility?

### The Problem with Current Platforms
- **Instagram/TikTok**: Entertainment and vanity metrics, no real utility
- **Discord**: Great for chat, but not for organizing real-world activities
- **GroupMe**: Just messaging, no tools or discovery
- **Facebook Groups**: Outdated, cluttered, not mobile-first
- **Craigslist/Facebook Marketplace**: Utility without social trust

### HIVE's Sweet Spot
```
SOCIAL UTILITY = Real Connections + Practical Solutions + Campus Trust
```
